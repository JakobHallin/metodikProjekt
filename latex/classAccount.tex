\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a user\textquotesingle{}s trading account, including balance and stock holdings. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classAccount}{Account}} class allows buying and selling stocks, updating the account balance, and managing the list of \mbox{\hyperlink{classStock}{Stock}} objects held by the account.
